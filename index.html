Pub Golf Rules

How to Play

Pub Golf is a drinking game where participants move from pub to pub (or "hole to hole"), with each pub having a designated drink and "par" value.

Basic Rules

  • Par - The target number of sips to finish your drink
  • Scoring - Lower scores are better, just like in golf
  • Hole in One - Finishing your drink in one go
  • Stroke - Each sip counts as one stroke
  • Penalties - Add strokes for spillages or incomplete drinks

Scoring Examples

Par 4 drink:
- Finish in 3 sips = -1 (one under par)
- Finish in 4 sips = 0 (par)
- Finish in 5 sips = +1 (one over par)

Penalties

  • Spillage - +1 stroke penalty
  • Not finishing - +2 stroke penalty
  • Wrong drink - +2 stroke penalty
  • Skipping a hole - Automatic maximum score (+3 over par)

Winner

The player with the lowest total score at the end of all holes is the winner!
